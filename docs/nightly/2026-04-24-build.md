## Why not the strict top-of-queue item (0d)?

Strict "top unblocked" is **0d — interaction-tests-critical-paths**
(`e2e/flows/*.spec.ts` for signup-to-paid, admin-roster, dismissal,
viewer-pin, branding-upgrade-gate). The current smoke sweep
(`e2e/smoke.spec.ts`, landed 2026-04-21) explicitly documents why those
flows are not yet runnable in the existing wrangler-dev harness:

> Seeded authenticated coverage (a real admin on a real tenant subdomain,
> and a real PLATFORM_ADMIN on the platform host) is out of scope for this
> smoke sweep. It requires:
>   - A tenant-slug host override (wrangler dev serves localhost only; dev
>     subdomains like `smoke-<ts>.localhost` aren't reachable from the
>     Playwright baseURL without /etc/hosts or a proxy).
>   - A seeded tenant with billing bypassed (signup ends at Stripe checkout
>     in the real flow).

Standing up that harness (Host-header proxy in the Playwright fixture +
billing-bypass seed) is itself a multi-night workstream. Shipping partial
flow specs where most tests are `.fixme`'d on the same blocker would be
worse than nothing — the queue's own quality rule says "Codifying a bug as
'passing' is worse than no test."

Workstream 4 (ops-runbook) is the next pilot-blocker on the P1 list, is
docs-only (zero risk of breaking the gate), and delivers the kind of
artifact a school admin can print and tape to the front desk on day one.
A follow-up queue item is added for the 0d seeded-flow harness.
